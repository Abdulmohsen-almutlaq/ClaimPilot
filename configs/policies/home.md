# Home Insurance Policy — Standard Terms

## HOME-001: Fire and smoke damage
Damage to the insured dwelling and its contents caused by fire, smoke, or
lightning is covered up to the policy coverage limit of $150,000 per incident.

## HOME-002: Water damage from burst pipes
Sudden and accidental water damage caused by burst pipes, plumbing failure, or
appliance leaks is covered. Gradual leaks and damage resulting from deferred
maintenance are excluded.

## HOME-003: Flood exclusion
Damage caused by external flooding, storm surge, or rising groundwater is
excluded from this policy and requires separate flood insurance. Claims for
flood damage must be rejected.

## HOME-004: Burglary and theft
Theft of personal property from the insured dwelling following forced entry is
covered. A police report is required. Coverage for jewelry, watches, and
collectibles is capped at $5,000 per item unless separately scheduled.

## HOME-005: Roof and storm damage
Storm damage to the roof from wind or hail is covered. Roofs older than 20 years
are covered at actual cash value rather than replacement cost.

## HOME-006: Deductible
A deductible of $1,000 applies to each claim. The payout for an approved claim
equals the assessed loss minus the deductible, never exceeding the coverage limit.

## HOME-007: Liability for guest injury
Bodily injury to guests occurring on the insured property is covered up to
$100,000 per occurrence, including medical expenses and legal costs.

## HOME-008: Unoccupancy limitation
If the dwelling is left unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days, theft and
water damage coverage is suspended until occupancy resumes.
