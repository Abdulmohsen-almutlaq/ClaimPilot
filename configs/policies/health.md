# Health Insurance Policy — Standard Terms

## HEALTH-001: Hospitalization
Inpatient hospitalization, including room, board, surgery, and intensive care,
is covered up to the policy coverage limit of $50,000 per policy year after
pre-authorization, except in emergencies.

## HEALTH-002: Emergency care
Emergency room treatment for acute conditions, accidents, and injuries is
covered without pre-authorization. Non-emergency use of the emergency room is
subject to a $250 copayment.

## HEALTH-003: Outpatient and specialist visits
Outpatient consultations, specialist visits, and diagnostic tests such as blood
work, X-ray, and MRI are covered at 80% of the contracted rate after the annual
deductible is met.

## HEALTH-004: Prescription drugs
Prescription medications on the policy formulary are covered with tiered
copayments. Non-formulary drugs require prior authorization and medical
justification from the prescribing physician.

## HEALTH-005: Exclusions — cosmetic procedures
Cosmetic and elective aesthetic procedures, including surgery performed solely
to improve appearance, are excluded unless reconstructive following a covered
accident or illness. Such claims must be rejected.

## HEALTH-006: Pre-existing conditions waiting period
Treatment for pre-existing conditions diagnosed before the policy effective
date is subject to a 12-month waiting period from policy inception.

## HEALTH-007: Preventive care
Annual checkups, screenings, and immunizations are covered in full with no
deductible or copayment.

## HEALTH-008: Claim submission window
Health claims must be submitted within 90 days of the date of service, together
with itemized bills and treatment records.
