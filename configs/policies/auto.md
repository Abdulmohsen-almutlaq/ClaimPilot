# Auto Insurance Policy — Standard Terms

## AUTO-001: Collision coverage
Collision damage to the insured vehicle is covered when the vehicle collides with
another vehicle or object, including rear-end collision, side impact, and single-car
accidents. Coverage applies up to the policy coverage limit of $25,000 per incident,
less the deductible stated on the declarations page.

## AUTO-002: Deductible
A deductible of $500 applies to each collision or comprehensive claim. The payout
for an approved claim equals the assessed repair or replacement cost minus the
deductible, and never exceeds the coverage limit.

## AUTO-003: Theft and vandalism
Theft of the insured vehicle, attempted theft, and vandalism damage are covered
under comprehensive coverage. A police report filed within 48 hours of discovering
the theft or vandalism is required for the claim to be eligible.

## AUTO-004: Exclusions — commercial use and racing
Damage sustained while the vehicle is used for commercial delivery, ride-sharing,
or participation in racing or speed contests is excluded from coverage. Claims
arising from such use must be rejected.

## AUTO-005: Exclusions — driving under the influence
Claims for accidents that occur while the driver is under the influence of alcohol
or drugs are excluded and must be rejected, regardless of fault.

## AUTO-006: Reporting window
Accidents must be reported within 30 days of the incident date. Claims reported
after 30 days require additional documentation explaining the delay and are
subject to individual review.

## AUTO-007: Rental car reimbursement
While a covered vehicle is under repair for an approved collision claim, rental
car costs are reimbursed up to $40 per day for a maximum of 20 days.

## AUTO-008: Windshield and glass
Windshield chips and glass breakage repairs are covered without applying the
deductible when repair (not replacement) is possible.
